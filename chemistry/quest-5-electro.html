Side Quest 5.1: Redox Reactions

Oxidation and reduction

Historical Context

Alessandro Volta (1800) - Invented the first battery (Voltaic pile).

Michael Faraday (1830s) - Established laws of electrolysis.

John Daniell (1836) - Developed the Daniell cell, improving battery stability.

Redox Fundamentals

Oxidation: Loss of electrons (LEO)

Reduction: Gain of electrons (GER)

Oxidation state: Assigned charge on atom

Real-World Applications

Batteries - Convert chemical energy to electrical energy via redox
Corrosion Prevention - Understanding rusting helps prevent metal degradation
Fuel Cells - Efficient energy conversion for clean technology
