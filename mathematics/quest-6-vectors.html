Side Quest 6.1: Vector Operations

Add, subtract, dot product

Vector Operations

Addition: a + b = (a₁+b₁, a₂+b₂)

Dot product: a·b = |a||b|cosθ = a₁b₁ + a₂b₂

Cross product: a×b (magnitude only)

Magnitude: |a| = √(a₁² + a₂²)
